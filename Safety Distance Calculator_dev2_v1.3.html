1. The Core Formula (Annex H)

D = (K √ó T) + dds + Z

  • K: Approach Speed (Usually 1600 mm/s). See Annex H.3.
  • T: Total Stopping Time (See section 2 below).
  • dds: Reach Distance (Depth Penetration). Depends on device type (Annex I).
  • Z: Supplemental factors (Measurement error, brake wear, etc.). See Annex H.6.

2. Total Stopping Time (T)

Annex H.4

T = Td + Ti + Tc + Ts + Tscm

  • Td (Device): Reaction time of the device. Source: Supplier Specification.
  • Ti (Interface): Reaction time of the safety interface. Source: Component Datasheet.
  • Tc (Control): Reaction time of the control system. Source: System Design/Config.
  • Ts (Machine): Physical stop time of the machine. Source: Stop-time Measurement.
  • Tscm (Monitor): Safety factor for degradation of braking system.
